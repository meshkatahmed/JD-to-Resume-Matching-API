Job Description: As an AI/ML Engineer at mPower, you will join our core engineering team to build, optimize, 
and maintain production-ready machine learning solutions across our enterprise portfolio. Your work will focus on 
expanding our core capabilities in Natural Language Processing (NLP), predictive modeling, and data-driven automation. 
This role is ideal for a versatile engineer who thrives on solving complex language and structural data challenges in a 
low-profile, high-impact commercial environment.

Job Responsibilities:
Fine-tune language and predictive models using custom datasets.
Optimize algorithms to process mixed language unstructured data pipelines.
Build and query graph-structured databases, relational networks, and vector repositories.
Construct clean, automated backend pipelines for multi-modal inputs.
Design, optimize, manage, secure bidirectional API to link AI/ML models with active enterprise solutions.
Run regular audits using semantic scoring metrics to track factual consistency and prevent system hallucinations.
Conduct technical stress testing to verify compliance.
Conduct code reviews, provide constructive feedback and mentor junior team members.
